About Amanten Senior High School

Category: B

Type: Public Senior High School

Location: Amantin, Atebubu, Bono East Region, Ghana

Description: Amanten Senior High School is a co-educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. With a strong focus on academic excellence, discipline, and character development, our school aims to empower students to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Performance Record: Amanten Senior High School has consistently achieved impressive results in national examinations, with a pass rate of 90% in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) over the past five years.

Facilities: Our school boasts modern facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms, science laboratories, a library, and sports facilities.

Mission: To provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth.
